What to Do in Laayoune at Night? Discover the Best Experiences and Restaurants

Laayoune, the pearl of the Sahara and a jewel of the Atlantic, captivates travelers with its breathtaking landscapes and peaceful atmosphere. But what can you do in Laayoune at night after a day of excursions? Discover the best nighttime activities, must-visit restaurants, and a secret spot not to be missed!

An Unforgettable Day in Laayoune with Laayoune Beyond Horizons

To make the most of your stay, start with a 4x4 excursion that takes you through Laayoune’s most stunning natural sites.

Departure from Laayoune – 10:00 AM

Your driver-guide will pick you up from your accommodation for a unique adventure through the desert and the Atlantic coast.

The White Dune – A Unique Natural Wonder

The first stop is at the White Dune, a spectacular natural site where the sand merges into a turquoise lagoon. This spot is perfect for admiring the scenery and capturing unforgettable photos.

Imlili Basins – A Secret of the Desert

Next, head to Imlili, a fascinating site where freshwater pools host fish in the middle of the desert. This incredible ecosystem never fails to amaze visitors.

Porto Rico Beach – Ultimate Relaxation

The journey continues to Porto Rico Beach, a peaceful paradise where the ocean meets the dunes. This is the perfect spot for a walk along the golden sand and to soak in the serenity.

Lunch at Talh el Hamar Restaurant – 1:00 PM

A delicious break awaits at Talh el Hamar Restaurant, known for its local specialties. Enjoy fresh fish accompanied by authentic Saharan flavors.

Return to Laayoune – 5:00 PM

After a day full of discoveries, return to Laayoune with memorable experiences and stunning pictures.
