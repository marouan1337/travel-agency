Faq

Find answers to the most common questions about our Sahara Desert adventures in Laayoune.

What should I bring for the desert adventure?

For your Sahara Desert adventure, it’s important to pack lightweight and comfortable clothing that can protect you from the sun during the day and keep you warm at night. Don’t forget sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat to shield you from the sun. A camera to capture the stunning desert views is essential. If you're camping, bring a sleeping bag suitable for cooler desert nights. Also, a water bottle is crucial to stay hydrated during the trip.

How long is the camel ride?

Our camel rides offer various durations depending on the experience you choose. Typically, rides last between 1 to 3 hours, allowing you to fully immerse yourself in the desert landscape. Longer rides can also be arranged if you'd like a more extensive adventure, offering you a deeper connection with the vast Sahara Desert.

Is camping safe in the Sahara Desert?

Yes, camping in the Sahara Desert with Laayoune Beyond Horizons is completely safe. Our team ensures that all camping sites are set up in secure, well-established areas with proper facilities. We provide experienced guides who are familiar with the terrain, ensuring your safety at all times. We also take all necessary precautions to guarantee your comfort, including providing reliable tents, sleeping arrangements, and adequate supplies for your stay.
