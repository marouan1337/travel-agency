CATAMARAN TRIP IN THE GREAT BAY OF LAAYOUNE

Catamaran Catamaran Catamaran

About

Enjoy this catamaran trip to discover the great bay of Laayoune and its wildlife, such as pink flamingos and dolphins.

Your catamaran journey begins with boarding from the nautical base. Once aboard, our friendly crew will take care of you, heading to the famous Dragon Island. During the journey, you can learn to navigate the catamaran and enjoy hot and cold drinks. Upon arrival, you will anchor and disembark on the paradisiacal Dragon Island. Take this moment to relax, swim, stroll around, or climb to the top for a breathtaking view of the entire island and see the pink flamingos. On the way back, you'll have a fruit appetizer, carrot cake, and drinks offered by our crew. With a bit of luck, we might see dolphins approaching the catamaran.

What to expect:

Our friendly crew will welcome you at the base, explain the circuit and safety instructions, then board the catamaran to start the experience by exploring Laayoune Bay. The captain will invite you to learn to navigate the catamaran. Upon reaching Dragon Island, you'll disembark to explore this wild island, home to pink flamingos and other migratory birds. You can swim or climb to the top for breathtaking views of the entire island. When you return to the catamaran, the crew will offer you a fruit aperitif, cake, and hot and cold drinks. On the way, you might see dolphins approaching the catamaran to play (weather permitting).

Friendly Note: Cancellation Policy – Catamaran Excursion

  • The latest time to cancel without penalty is before 8:00 PM the day prior to the excursion.
  • In case of late cancellation or no-show, the full amount will be charged, unless we are able to find another client to take the spot.
  • During high season and school holidays, we sometimes operate with a waiting list.
  • Prompt communication from your side in case of cancellation allows us to better organize and offer the excursion to other guests.

We offer sailing adventures with a visit to the island (this is not a classic cruise). If weather conditions are not favorable, we will cancel the excursion and you will receive a full refund.

Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.
